Box League - Mixed Singles Matches

For all players over 16.

Players will be grouped into boxes (typically 4–5 players per group), and each player plays every other player in their box during the round.

!! IMPORTANT: League of Areté - This league is exclusively for player members committed to achieving Excellence in Action and can demonstrate Practical Wisdom (Phronesis) to ensure all matches are arranged and completed within the round deadline.

How It Works

  • The Virtue of Commitment: You are expected to complete all matches by being proactive, dependable, and courteous - essential to maintaining league's noble goal (kalon) - which is flourishing (Eudaimonia) of the community.
  • Join anytime: New players can join at the start of any round or during the round if there is availability in a box.
  • Box Grouping: Players will be grouped based on past performance or known ability.
  • Match Scheduling: Players contact each other directly to arrange matches.
  • Scoring: Best of 3 standard sets, OR 2 sets with a 10-point match tie break at 1 all.
  • Points: Win = 3 points, Loss = 1
  • Promotion/Relegation: Top players move up a box, bottom may move down (if enough participants).
  • Reporting Results: Post match scores using a google form: Tennis Nerds League - Submit Scores - Google Forms
  • WTN (Optional): If you want your matches to count towards your World Tennis Number (WTN), you can also submit the result through your LTA Advantage account. LTA Self-Submission
