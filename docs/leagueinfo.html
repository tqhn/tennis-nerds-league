Welcome to the Tennis Nerds League

Our box league format ensures competitive matches at your skill level, flexible scheduling, and a friendly atmosphere where players of all abilities can improve their game and make new friends.

Join the League LTA Portal (Enter Results)

Mixed Singles Box League

For all players over 18.

Players will be grouped into boxes (typically 5-7 players per group), and each player plays every other player in their box during the round.

!! IMPORTANT: League of Areté - This league is exclusively for player members committed to achieving Excellence in Action and can ensure all matches are arranged and completed within the round deadline.

How It Works

  • Join anytime: New players can join at the start of any round or during the round if there is availability in a box.
  • Box Grouping: Players will be grouped based on past performance or known ability.
  • Match Scheduling: Players contact each other directly to arrange matches.
  • Scoring: Best of 3 standard sets
  • Points: Win = 3 points, Loss = 1
  • Promotion/Relegation: Players move up/down a group based on their WTN rating.
  • Reporting Results: Post match scores using a google form: Tennis Nerds League - Submit Scores
  • WTN (Optional): Submit through your LTA Advantage account. LTA Self-Submission

🏆 Commitment Champion Award

All players completing 100% of their matches in a round are automatically entered into a prize draw to win the round's designated prize.
